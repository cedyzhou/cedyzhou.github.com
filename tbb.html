TianBaoBao

TianBaoBao is used to reserve the tourism parks managed by the group for parent-child interactive activities and to sell group brand green crops online. Members can book activities and purchase products through their membership cards to accumulate points.

  • Role: UX & UI Design/Research<
  • Context: Internal Project
  • Date: October 2014

Our Approach

When beginning the design of the APP, our team constructed an outline of the necessary UX practices which we felt were the most necessary for a successful design. The practices that were defined in this meeting included:

  • Content Inventory
  • Review of Site Analytics
  • Card Sorting Priortization with the Line of Business
  • Competitive Analysis
  • Site Mapping
  • Wireframing

To begin the redesign process, and ensure that our team was focusing on the correct sections, we had to develop a deep understanding for the current status to understand the pain points and sections that required the most work.

Final Takeaways

The app design was my first professional experience within a UI/UX design team. I was exposed to a variety of new UX practices, and was able to gain detailed insight as to how operates within the digital field.
